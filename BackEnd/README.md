# Who is "ma'am"?

"Ma'am" is a contraction of "madam" used as a polite form of address for a woman.  
- Formal/respectful tone (common in customer service, military, etc.).  
- Some people prefer alternatives (their name, "Ms.", or no honorific) depending on context.

Examples:
- "Excuse me, ma'am, can I help you?"  
- "Good morning, ma'am."
